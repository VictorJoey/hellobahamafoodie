FAQ
Got any questions?

Here’s where you can see the questions people ask the most. You could almost call these questions “frequently asked”.

Do you cook completely plant-based food?

Yes, we do, all of our dishes are void of meat, meat derivatives, dairy, and eggs.

Do you provide a meal prep service?

At this time we do not but we do cater to private and corporate events.

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, we do for some of our dishes, they are indicated during the ordering process.

Are your dishes NON-GMO?

All of our dishes are NON-GMO we pride ourselves on making delicious food from whole foods and fresh ingredients.

Do you cook with allergens?

Some of our dishes contain nuts but we ensure that you are aware of them during the ordering process.

Send it our way and we’ll answer ASAP!

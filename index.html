We are a digital pop-up restaurant serving delicious plant-based meals on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays currently. To better serve you, we have created an online process that allows you to order our meals seamlessly.


How Does It work?


Step 1: Select the featured meal or meals for the week. (You can order all three dishes one time)

Step 2: Specify any add-on options you want to be added to your meal.

Step 3: Choose Mobile Pickup or Delivery.

Step 4: Make payment and place your order for one meal or all meals for the week.

Step 5: Lunch is served! If you chose pickup, we will see you at 11:30 at the Southwest Plaza Carmichael Rd. If you choose delivery, our driver will contact you with further details.
